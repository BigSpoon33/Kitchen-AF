# Chicken Stir Fry

A quick and healthy weeknight dinner packed with vegetables and lean protein.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced thin
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Prep**: Cut chicken into thin strips. Slice vegetables.
2. **Sauce**: Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and ginger in a small bowl.
3. **Cook chicken**: Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok over high heat. Cook chicken 4-5 minutes until golden. Remove and set aside.
4. **Cook vegetables**: Add remaining oil. Stir-fry broccoli, peppers, and snap peas for 3-4 minutes.
5. **Combine**: Return chicken to wok. Add garlic and sauce. Toss for 1 minute.
6. **Serve**: Serve over rice or noodles.

## Notes

- Can substitute tofu for a vegetarian version
- Add chili flakes for heat
- Prep all ingredients before starting - stir fry moves fast!
