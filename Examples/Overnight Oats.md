# Overnight Oats

No-cook oatmeal that's ready when you wake up. Perfect for meal prep.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk (any kind)
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Toppings
- Fresh berries
- Sliced banana
- Chopped nuts
- Nut butter

## Instructions

1. **Combine**: Add oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, maple syrup, vanilla, and salt to a jar or container.
2. **Mix**: Stir well to combine everything.
3. **Refrigerate**: Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 4 hours).
4. **Serve**: In the morning, stir and add your favorite toppings.

## Notes

- Lasts up to 5 days in the fridge (without toppings)
- Make a batch of 5 jars for the whole week
- Can be eaten cold or microwaved for 1-2 minutes
